About me

What you need to know

My name is Lorin. I was born in 1991, which makes me 22 years of age. I'm currently living in Leuven, Belgium where I go to college. Next year I'll (hopefully) have my bachelor's degree in project and event management. However, over the last few years I have come to the realization this is not a field I would necessarily love to work in. That's why I have something completely different planned for next year, which I can't tell you anything about just yet.
Feel free to take a look around my website. If you want to talk, get in touch! I'm always on the lookout for interesting and open-minded people. You can find my details at the bottom of this page.

What keeps me up at night?

  • Music.

    I have been writing and producing drum & bass music for the last 5 years. Aditionally, I write music for video games and film. Analog synthesizers, compressors,microphones and pre-amps get my blood pumping.

  • Formula 1.

    The greatest sport on earth. Whenever I hear the engines roar up, smell the fuel and burning rubber, my heart starts to race.

  • Technology.

    I build and dissect computers. I am always on the lookout for new ways to incorporate technology in my life.

  • Friends.

    Probably by sending me pictures of the bottle of rum they're about to attack.
